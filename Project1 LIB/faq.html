Frequently Asked Questions

Q.1 What are the general Rules and Guidelines of the Library? 

Ans. Please Click here to know more details.

Q.2 How can I become member of the Library? 

Ans. There is no form is required to become Library Member. New Member details will be added in the Library database once your academic registration is done.

Q.3 Is there any separate Library Card will be issued for using the Library?

Ans. There is no separate card issued for members to use the Library. Institute Identity card (I-card) is sufficient to enter the Library and for referring the collections. Bonafide members only will have borrowing privileges.

Q.4 How many books can I borrow?

Ans

Category of Members Entitlement Period of Loan
Faculty/Visiting Faculty 06 Books/Documents for a semester
Research Associate 04 Books/Documents for a semester
Research Scholar 04 14 Days
U.G. /P.G. Student 04 14 Days
U.G. Students (SC/ST) Book Bank 06 Books for a semester
U.G. Students Book Bank 04 Books for a semester

Q.5 Can I borrow books by sending I-card through someone? 

Ans. No. The borrower has to come and get it issued himself/herself.

Q.6 Can I renew issued book(s)? 

Ans. Yes, The borrower can reissue the books after 14 days.

Q.7 I have lost Library Book. What is the procedure for clearance?

Ans. Replacement: You can replace the latest version of the Book Lost with processing fee Rs.150/-.

Q.8 I am going on academic Leave or temporarily withdrawing semester. Do I need to return the books which I borrowed?

Ans. Yes. Library may recall the document for any purpose. It is recommended to return all the books before proceeding on long Leave for optimum use of the Library resources by all.

Q.9 How do I access E-resources subscribed by the Library? Do I need any separate login/password? 

Ans. e-resources are accessible through IP authenticated within the campus. There is no need of any separate login/password to access these. You may also contact the concerned I/c or the Librarian to know more details.

Q.10 Can I access E-resources during Off-Campus?

Ans. No. You can access E-resources on Institute IPs only.

Q. 11 How do I get my clearance from the Library?

Ans. You have to visit library and submit an application to get clearance. After checking records library staff will know your library dues, if any. After clearing the dues, you will get signed no dues certificate from library.
