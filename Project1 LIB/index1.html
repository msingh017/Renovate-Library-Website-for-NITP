The Central library of this Institute is one of oldest engineering library of its own region, came into existence along with the establishment of pleaders survey training school in 1886 which was subsequently promoted of Bihar College of Engineering Patna in 1924. Later on this REC was upgraded to NIT on 28.01.2004 and came under umbrella of MHRD, Govt. of India. From its inception, the Central Library search, select, analysis, procure, process, arrange and retrieve library materials in accordance thrust area of Institute. The library possesses hybrid collection (print and electronic) of learning resource materials in the field of science, engineering, management, social science, language and humanities. The Central Library which acts as a reservoir of knowledge can be accessed by entire community of NIT Patna. The Central library has automated its routine activities through library management software “LIBSYS”.

The Central library of the institute owns a vast stock of over 75,000 books catering to the needs of all the relevant fields of engineering. It has a resourceful stock of over 10,000 computer related books and clearly demarcated section for every engineering branch and architecture. In addition to the engineering pertinent books, the central library is self-sufficient in a vivid category of other business and current affairs related magazines, pamphlets and journals which includes TIME and NEWSWEEK. The Central Library is fully automated and all operations are performed with the help of Libsys-Library Automation Software. The students can issue a sufficient no. of books for use throughout the semester and an additional no. of books for special reference.
